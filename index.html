About the Superscript Generator

The Superscript Generator is a free online tool that transforms standard text characters into their superscript equivalents. Superscript text appears slightly above the normal line and is smaller in size, commonly used in mathematical expressions, scientific notation, footnotes, and chemical formulas.

Example Conversion:
Normal: x^2 + y^2 = z^2
Superscript: x² + y² = z²

How to Use the Converter

1. Type or paste your text in the "Normal Text" field above.
2. Click "Convert to Superscript" to transform your text.
3. Copy the superscript result using the "Copy Output" button.
4. Paste the superscript text into your document, website, or application.

Supported Characters

Our converter supports all digits (0-9), lowercase letters (a-z), uppercase letters (A-Z), and common mathematical symbols (+, -, =, (, )). Characters not in this set will remain unchanged. The tool preserves spaces, line breaks, and formatting.
